| 2026-07-09 | P8 / sim | **Diagnosed the real blocker: loop DEAD TIME, not gains.** The honest-lag gains (Kp=60 Ki=500 Kd=8) still limit-cycled on hardware (~6-7 Hz, w_common railing ±30 while wheels only reach ±7, duty saturated) - so the sim was still optimistic.
